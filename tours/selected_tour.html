Locations

We'll start off at Gurog, walking down a gentle path throughout the city. There will be landmarks scattered throughout the walk, which will be accompanied by an audio narration. On the second day, we'll take a trolley to the countryside in Svitz, where a good friend of mine will be lecturing us on the significance of the site. On the third and final day, we will congregate at the local church in Shamona and explore how the long and bloody history of Vorscum affected the local view on religion and morals.

Dates and times

On the start of the tour, we will meet at the cafe in Gurog at 12:00 on the 5th of July. We will meet at this cafe at this exact time on the 6th and the 7th too, and the tour will officially end at 4pm on the 7th.
