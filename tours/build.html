plan your tour

We take the quality of everything hosted here at the Travel Palace very seriously, and the tours are no exception. Whether this is your first time making a tour or your hundredth, these guidelines must be followed:

  • Make all arangements ahead of time. Though it is possible to cancel tours, the process may take some time, and it is best to ensure everything is in order.
  • Create a video covering your tour. The video should be at least one minute long and mention any pertinent details. Creating captions for it is not required, but highly recommended. Click here to see a gif clip for reference
  • Submit your tour proposal at least two weeks in advance. This is to allow for ample time to change any details of the tour, and to ensure there is no backlog of tour proposals to shift through. We recieve many proposals each day, but we endevour to reach you withing 2-3 business days.

Submit your tour

Once you have sufficiently planned your tour, you will need to email us atTravelPalace@gmail.com.

As mentioned in the previous section, we will endevour to get back to you withing 2-3 business days. If we have not contacted you after 5 days, please email us again, or find more ways to contact us at the Contact Us page hosted on the homepage.

Should you have any more questions, consult the compiled PDF document downloadable here, and if you still have any questions, refer to the Help or Contact Us pages, hosted on the homepage.

You must specify price per person (preferably in USD), the start date of the tour, and the maximum size of the group. These are the details that will go on your tour card. Other details that will be displayed are the exact locations you will be touring through, what type of tour it is (audio, walking, etc), and whether it is disability friendly.
